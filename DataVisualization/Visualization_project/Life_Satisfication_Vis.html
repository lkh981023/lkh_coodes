How does the working hour effect on the happiness index🤔

This section shows the overview of relationship between working hour and happiness index.And the cluster analysis result will also be shown in this section. I'm apply the cluster analysis while using KMeans algorithms with K=3🧐

In this section you will also see the ratio: work-life ratio🤔. We define this ratio as:

Working-life ratio = Average annual working hour / Life satisfaction index

Assuming the working hour is relatively fixed, the higher ratio means that this country’s working-life balance cannot be achieved well since they have low life satisfaction index...😞

In the world...🌐

Nowadays most of people are busy with their work and work-life balance is a goal that we always want to acheived. How does the working hour effect on the happiness index around the world? The graph shows the relationship between average annual working hour and average life satisfication index record from 2010 to 2017.
